How to copy Slack messages:
1. Open Slack and go to the channel
2. Scroll up to load more messages
3. Select all the text and copy (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A won't work — just drag to select)
4. Paste below

Use Slack's built-in export:
1. In Slack, open the channel you want
2. Click the channel name at the top → Settings
3. Or go to slack.com/admin/export (workspace admins only)
4. Export as JSON, open the file, copy the messages array
5. Paste below
